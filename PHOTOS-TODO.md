# Photo upload checklist

Colorways of **in-stock** devices that have no real photo yet (they
currently fall back to the procedural render — nothing is broken, these are
just the photos worth shooting first). For each one:

1. Save the photo as `public/photos/<device-slug>__<color-slug>.jpg`
   (exact double-underscore naming, e.g. `public/photos/ipad-9th-gen__space-gray.jpg`).
2. Add the key `"<device-slug>::<color-slug>"` to `src/data/color-photos.json`.

The site picks them up automatically. Devices are ordered by stock on hand,
so working top-down covers the most units fastest.
